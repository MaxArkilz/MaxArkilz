RECIPE: Chicken Curry

**TAGS: chicken, rice**

Servings: 6
_
SPICE BLEND:
Ground_Coriander 1.5 tsp
Ground_Cumin 1 tsp
Tumeric 0.5 tsp
Fennel_Seeds,crushed 0.5 tsp
Cinnamon 0.5 tsp
Pepper 0.5 tsp
Ground_Mustard 0.25 tsp
Ground_Cloves 0.25 tsp
_
CURRY:
Olive_Oil 2 tbsp
Onion 1 cup
Garlic 4 cloves
Ginger 1 tbsp
Chicken_Broth 1 cup
Diced_Tomatoes 0.75 cup
Chicken_Breast,diced 1.5 lbs
Cornstarch 1 tsp
Water 2 tsp
Heavy_Cream 0.33 cup
_
Instructions:
1. Mix SPICE BLEND together, set aside.
2. Saute ONION in OIL for 4-6 minutes on medium-high heat.
3. Add GARLIC and GINGER, saute for 1 minutes, then add SPICE BLEND.
4. Add CHICKEN BROTH and TOMATOES, bring to a boil. Reduce heat, simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Blend mixture until well pureed. Return to skillet on medium-high heat.
6. Add CHICKEN, simmer until cooked through.
7. Mix CORNSTARCH and WATER and add to sauce.
8. Serve over basmati rice.

RECIPE: Arepas

**TAGS: chicken**

Servings: 4
_
CHICKEN AVOCADO MIX:
Canned_Chicken 2 can
Avocado 1 whole
Mayonnaise 1.5 tbsp
Lime_Juice 0.5 tbsp
Red_Bell_Pepper 0.5 whole
Onion 0.5 whole
Jalapeno_Pepper 0.5 whole
Garlic 1 tsp
Salt 0.5 tsp
Pepper 0.5 tsp
_
AREPAS:
Corn_Flour 1.5 cup
Warm_Water 1.5 cup
Salt 0.5 tsp
_
Instructions:
1. Mix all CHICKEN AVOCADO MIX ingredients together in a large bowl.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
3. Mix WARM WATER with SALT and kneed together with CORN FLOUR until smooth.
4. Divide dough into even pieces, shape into disks.
5. Sear arepas in skillet on medium heat.
6. Cook for 15-20 minutes in the oven. Cut them in half and fill with the mix.
